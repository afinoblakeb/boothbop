BoothBop Support

We're happy to help

BoothBop turns your phone into a photo booth: four photos back to back, then a strip, GIF, or video. Here are answers to common questions.

Need a hand? Email support@boothbop.com and we'll get back to you.

How do I take photos?

Prop up your phone in selfie mode, tap Take Photos, and BoothBop counts down and snaps four shots in a row. Strike a pose between each one!

Where are my photos? Are they private?

Everything stays on your device. BoothBop has no accounts and no servers — your photos are never uploaded and never leave your phone. Each session is saved to a private on-device gallery you fully control.

How do I save or share a strip, GIF, or video?

After your shots, pick a format (Strip, GIF, or Video) and tap Save / Share. You can save it to your device or send it through the share sheet (Messages, AirDrop, social, and more).

How do I delete my photos?

Open My Photos, where you can delete any individual session or tap Clear all to wipe everything from your device.

Does it work offline?

Yes. Once installed, BoothBop works without a connection — take photos and export strips, GIFs, and videos anywhere.

I used to have "PhotoBlast" — what happened?

BoothBop is the same photo booth, renamed. If you have the old PhotoBlast shortcut, you can delete it and use BoothBop instead.

The camera won't start

BoothBop needs camera permission to work. If you denied it, enable camera access for BoothBop in your device Settings, then reopen the app. Make sure no other app is using the camera.
