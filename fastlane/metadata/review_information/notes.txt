NO LOGIN REQUIRED. There is no account, sign-up, or paywall to use the app.
The full booth is available immediately on launch—no demo credentials needed.

WHAT THE APP DOES
BoothBop is a self-contained native photo-booth app. It uses the live device
camera (front-facing) to capture four photos on a countdown, then composites
them on-device into a photo strip (4x1 or 2x2), an animated GIF, and a short
looping video (encoded natively via AVFoundation/AVAssetWriter).

RE: GUIDELINE 4.2
The UI renders in an embedded WKWebView via Capacitor, but ALL assets are
bundled in the app binary—nothing loads from a remote URL and the web view
never points at a website. The camera, native video encoding, on-device
gallery, Photos-album save, and the native share sheet are driven by native
plugins. The app fully functions in Airplane Mode.

PRIVACY
No data is collected. Photos are captured and processed entirely on-device and
are never uploaded—no servers, accounts, analytics, ads, or third-party SDKs.
