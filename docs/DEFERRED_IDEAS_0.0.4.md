# Deferred Product Observations After 0.0.4 Review

These ideas surfaced during the final quality and distribution review. They are
not release commitments and are intentionally excluded from build `0.0.5`.
Each requires user evidence and its own feature brief.

1. **Reorder the four captured photos.** A lightweight drag-to-reorder step
   could rescue otherwise good sessions without another capture. Validate that
   users need ordering more often than it complicates the simple review screen.
2. **Optional short strip caption.** A single restrained line could make saved
   strips more personal. It must preserve exact print geometry, accessibility,
   and the default zero-setup flow.
3. **Favorite sessions in My Photos.** Useful only after repeat users accumulate
   enough local sessions for retrieval to become a real problem.
4. **Destination-aware share guidance.** The app could label recommended Post,
   Reel, and Story formats before opening the system share sheet. Avoid social
   SDKs or authentication unless user evidence clearly justifies them.
5. **App Preview video.** Turn the current screenshot storyboard into a short,
   truthful App Store preview after the release is stable. It should use actual
   recorded app interaction rather than simulated or invented UI.
6. **Campaign-specific product pages.** Friends, couples, and family moments may
   merit separate App Store creative sets after enough traffic exists to test
   conversion meaningfully.
